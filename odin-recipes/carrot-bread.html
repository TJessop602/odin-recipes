Carrot Bread

A low and round loaf filled with carrot and onion

This recipe is fairly unfussy as to the exact details, feel free to stray somewhat from the exact quantities give.

  • 2 medium carrots
  • 1 large onion
  • 2 tsp thyme (other herbs can also be added)
  • 2 tsp yeast
  • 3 cups flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 Tb oil (canola works well, olive is fine too)
  • 1 1/4 cups warm water
  1. Grate the carrots into fine shreads and cut up the onion finely
  2. Fry the carrot and onion in a pan with a dizzling of oil until the carrot shreads lighten in colour and the onion begin to turn gold. This is typically about 5 to 10 minutes on high heat.
  3. Combine all remaining ingredients and kneed them into a dough
  4. Leave this dough to stand for around 30 minutes to an hour
  5. Next, add the carrot and onion to the dough and kneed to combine
  6. Leave the dough in a warm place to rise
  7. Once you judge it to have risen enough bake at 200 C for 20 minutes, for the first 10 minutes cover the top of the loaf with a sheet of baking paper. This stops the crust from burning while we wait for the dough to bake all the way through. Remove this paper for the last 10 minutes
  8. Remove bread from oven and allow to cool
  9. Enjoy!
